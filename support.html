Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Setup

How do I create a Freeway Cards account? +
You can create an account by clicking "Sign Up" and using either Google OAuth or email registration. Choose between Individual or Business account types during signup.
What documents do I need for KYC verification? +
You'll need a government-issued ID (passport, driver's license, or national ID), proof of address (utility bill, bank statement, or lease agreement), and a selfie for identity verification.

Virtual Cards

How do I create a virtual card? +
Go to the Cards page, click "Create New Card", choose Visa or Mastercard, set your spending limit, and your card will be generated instantly with a real card number, CVV, and expiry date.
Can I set spending limits on my cards? +
Yes! You can set spending limits from $100 to $10,000 when creating a card, and modify them later in the card settings. You can also set daily, monthly, and per-transaction limits.
How do I freeze or unfreeze a card? +
In your Cards page or card details, click the "Freeze" or "Unfreeze" button. Frozen cards cannot be used for transactions until unfrozen.

Wallet & Payments

How do I add funds to my wallet? +
You can fund your wallet using cryptocurrency (BTC, USDT, ETH), bank transfers, or debit cards. Go to your Wallet page and click "Add Funds" to see all available options.
Are there fees for withdrawals? +
Yes, there's a $2.50 withdrawal fee. The fee is deducted from your withdrawal amount, so you'll receive the requested amount minus the fee.
